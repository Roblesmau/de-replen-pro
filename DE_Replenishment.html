    Store-to-store transfer suggestions ☁ Ledger shared across all users
    Finds SKUs sitting dead at one store and suggests moving them to another store where they'll sell. A SKU is "dead" if it has stock on hand, zero sales in 90 days, and hasn't been touched at that store for at least N days (adjustable below). Matches are ranked: exact-SKU first (destination is already selling this specific frame), then Brand+Type (destination is selling the category well). Uses the shared transfer ledger as the "last received" clock, with Shopify's updated_at as fallback. The Inventory tab's Store filter scopes source stores only — destinations are always considered across all stores (Brand/Type filters apply to both). Respects the Exceptions list and destination capacity, including the over-capacity tolerance and any units already committed by the current replenishment plan.
